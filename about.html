ABOUT US

Best Quality Products is a social enterprise empowering the society through chicken and business


Our unique one-stop shop model addresses the farmer's needs by providing all products and services under one roof


Our products include;

  • Feeds/Medication(inputs))
  • Chicken Meat
  • Eggs
  • Day Old Birds/One-Month-Old And Above

We also offer training and capacity building on poultry management and provide 24-hour veterinary extension services

Once the chickens are mature we buy them back from the farmer,process and sell the meat and eggs in our butcheries and restaurants that specialise in various tasty chicken meals
